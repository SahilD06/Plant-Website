About Our Database

Our plant database includes a total of 37 flora within Teli Gali, with 37 unique plant species identified. The data, which is 100% primary, has been carefully collected through field research and digitally documented.

All plant information — including names, types, and key attributes — is stored as text files in the Gist directory on GitHub for transparency and future reference.

How to Use

Use the search feature on our homepage to find detailed information about any plant in our database. Each plant entry includes:

  • Common and scientific names
  • Plant type and height
  • Lifespan and oxygen production level
  • Detailed description
  • High-quality images
  • Location information with interactive maps
  • External resources and video content

QR Code Access

Visit Teli Gali Garden & Park to experience our plant database in person! Each tree is equipped with a QR code that provides instant access to comprehensive plant information when scanned with your smartphone.

This innovative approach combines technology with nature education, making it easy for everyone to learn about the plants around them.
