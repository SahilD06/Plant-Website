About Our Database

Our plant database includes a total of 20 unique plant species, carefully curated to showcase a variety of botanical wonders. The data is 100% primary, collected through field research and digitally documented.

All plant information — including names, types, and key attributes — is stored as text files in the Gist directory on GitHub for transparency and future reference.

How to Use

Use the search feature on our homepage to find detailed information about any plant in our database. Each plant entry includes:

  • Common and scientific names
  • Detailed descriptions
  • High-quality images
  • External informational links
  • Educational video content via YouTube

QR Code Access

Visit Public Recreation Ground (ITC) Garden & Park to experience our plant database in person! Each tree is equipped with a QR code that provides instant access to comprehensive plant information when scanned with your smartphone.

This innovative approach combines technology with nature education, making it easy for everyone to learn about the plants around them.
