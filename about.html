Our Mission

We are a group of students who created this website as part of our field project to promote awareness about local plant life. Our goal is to make it easy for visitors to learn more about the trees and plants around them through technology.

By scanning the QR codes placed in Teli Gali Garden & Park, users can instantly access detailed information, photos, and interesting facts about each plant.

This project combines our interest in nature and web development to encourage more people to appreciate and understand the greenery in their surroundings.

Project Summary

Our project has developed a user-friendly webpage designed to guide users through detailed information about various plant species. The webpage provides essential details in the form of cards that includes plant's image, common name, scientific name, type, height, life span, oxygen level, and a comprehensive description. Additionally, users can access further information through a Google link and explore video perspectives via a YouTube link.

Plants Database
