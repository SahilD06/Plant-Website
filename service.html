Our section features plant information cards, each containing the following details: Plant image, Common Name, Scientific Name, Type, Height, Lifespan, Oxygen Production Level, Short Description, and links for further information on Google and relevant YouTube videos (if available).

What We Offer:

  • Comprehensive Plant Database: Access detailed information about 37 unique plant species found in Teli Gali Garden & Park
  • QR Code Integration: Scan QR codes placed near plants to instantly access their information on your smartphone
  • Detailed Plant Cards: Each plant entry includes images, common and scientific names, type, height, lifespan, and oxygen production levels
  • External Resources: Links to Google for additional information and YouTube videos for visual learning
  • Location Mapping: Interactive maps showing where each plant is located in the garden

QR Code Identification

Visit Teli Gali Garden & Park and discover our innovative QR code system! Each tree and plant in the garden is equipped with a unique QR code that provides instant access to comprehensive information.

How It Works:

  • Step 1: Locate a plant with a QR code tag in Teli Gali Garden & Park
  • Step 2: Open your smartphone camera or QR code scanner app
  • Step 3: Point your camera at the QR code
  • Step 4: Tap the notification to open the plant's information page
  • Step 5: Explore detailed information, images, and videos about the plant

This innovative approach combines technology with nature education, making it easy for everyone to learn about the plants around them while enjoying a walk in the garden.
