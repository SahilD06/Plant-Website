Plants Information

This section features plant information cards, each containing the following details: Plant Image, Common Name, Scientific Name, Type, Height, Lifespan, Oxygen Production Level, Short Description, and links for further information on Google and relevant YouTube videos (if available).

QR Identification

Each plant will be equipped with its own unique QR code. When you scan the QR code, you will instantly access detailed information specific to that plant, including its name, characteristics, care instructions, and more. This feature will simplify plant identification and enhance your learning experience, making it ideal for use in gardens, nurseries, and educational environments.
